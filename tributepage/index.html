About

Tahliah Debrett Barnett, more known by her stage-name—
FKA Twigs, is an British singer, songwriter, and dancer. They make music around the genres of avant-garde and electronic music. They currently have two studio albums, three LPS, and one mixtape.

Influence

FKA Twigs has been described as a "genre-bending artist", with create artistic direction and creativity. She is unafraid to let her emotion show through her music as she takes you on a journey through her love life, both good and the bad. She is able to relate to her audience, exploring themes of female sexuality and power. She has been nominated for and won awards for her work.
